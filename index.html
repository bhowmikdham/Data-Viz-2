McLaren: From Underdog to Champion

An interactive journey through McLaren's Formula 1 legacy, from their humble beginnings to periods of sheer dominance.

From the dream of its founder, New Zealander Bruce McLaren, in the 1960s, the McLaren Formula 1 team has forged a legacy of innovation, triumph, and resilience. This is a story of a team that has experienced soaring highs, becoming synonymous with legendary champions, and challenging lows that tested its spirit. This visualisation explores that journey through time, geography, and the drivers who made it all possible.

The Journey Through Time

The timeline below charts every season of McLaren's history, measured by their number of Grand Prix wins. It visually captures the story: the initial struggles, the first taste of success in the 70s, the absolute dominance of the late 80s with Senna and Prost, and the long, challenging period of rebuilding that has finally led to a resurgence in recent years.
